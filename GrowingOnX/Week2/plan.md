## Why this differs from Week 1

Week 1 was profile setup + a single big push (the pinned post). Week 2 starts the **rhythm** — a deliverable thread every Friday, ambient short posts and replies between. This is the cadence that compounds.

The pinned post stays pinned. Don't unpin it for a Friday thread unless the Friday thread is meaningfully better — which is rare in week 2.
