# Day 2 — Monday, 4 May 2026

**Theme:** Build the engine. Most of your early follower growth comes from *replies*, not your own posts. Today you build the system that makes daily replies sustainable.

## Today's actions

### 1. Build the reply watch list (45 min)
From the 30 accounts you followed yesterday, pick **15** whose audience overlaps with yours and who post regularly. These are your daily reply targets.

Save in `Week1/watch-list.md` with one line per account:
```
@handle — why they matter — typical post window (IST) — your angle for replying
```

The reply rule: **show up within 15 minutes** of their post, and contribute something substantive — a counter-example, a related paper, a question that pushes the idea forward, a real disagreement. Never flattery. Never "great post."

### 2. Set up X notifications for the watch list (15 min)
For each of the 15 watch-list accounts: profile → bell icon → "Notify me about all posts." This is what makes the 15-minute rule actually doable around your day job.

### 3. Follow the next 60 accounts (45 min)

**30 systems-thinkers / first-principles voices**, e.g.:
@vgr, @paulg, @balajis, @naval, @david_perell, @nntaleb, @michael_nielsen, @patrickc, @tobi, @benthompson, @noahpinion, @tylercowen, @rsbakker, @jasoncrawford, @fortelabs, @sapinker, @apenwarr, @robertskmiles, @vkhosla, @lessin, @pmarca, @dharmesh, @aaronson, @dwarkesh_sp, @eladgil, @ezraklein, @TashaArk, @byrnehobart, @StephenJokes, @michaelnotebook

**30 mid-sized adjacent (5K–50K) AI/agents accounts** you'll regularly reply to. Find these by checking the reply sections of your top-30 builders — the people consistently in those threads are your peer cohort.

### 4. Send your first 5 replies (30 min)
Pick 5 watch-list accounts. Find a recent post by each. Reply with substance.

This is reps practice — they don't need to land hard. The point is starting the muscle.

### 5. Set up a thread-idea capture system (15 min)
Pick one place (Apple Notes, Obsidian, a single file in this repo at `Week1/thread-ideas.md`) — anywhere you'll capture every "huh, that's interesting" moment from your day. By Wednesday you'll need 5 candidate pinned-post topics; you can't generate them on demand without a capture habit.
